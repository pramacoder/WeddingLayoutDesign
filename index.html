Our Story

Once Upon a Time in College...

William Saputra and Skyee Kanahaya were just two ordinary classmates at university, each lost in their own world of books, assignments, and dreams. But little did they know, fate had a different story in store for them. It all started in a crowded lecture hall where their eyes met for the first time.

Skyee, with her radiant smile and easy laugh, caught William’s attention immediately. He couldn’t quite explain it, but there was something magnetic about her. But as the days went by, they became mere acquaintances, sharing notes and exchanging casual conversations about class topics. Despite their friendly exchanges, there was something unspoken between them, a growing connection that neither of them dared to address.

The Wedding

The days leading up to their wedding were filled with joy and anticipation. Their love story was no longer just a fairy tale; it was their reality. Surrounded by friends, family, and loved ones, they vowed to cherish and support each other for the rest of their lives. Their wedding day was a reflection of their journey—a celebration of love, laughter, and all the little moments that had led them to this beautiful chapter.

And so, from the lecture halls of university to the cozy café by the lake, their love story continues to unfold. Now, with hearts full of gratitude and excitement, William and Skyee invite you to join them in celebrating the next chapter of their journey.
